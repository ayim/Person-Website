Appazur Social Media Rebranding

Design work to leverage Appazur's social media presence

Content Strategy, Prototyping, Visual Design

Overview

What is Appazur?

Appazur's core product is a student planner app. 1 tap contacts & info, a unified calendar, color coded assignments with daily reminders, and notifications via push, email, and SMS are just some of the key features that enable frictionless engagement of today's busy parents and students.

The Project

Build a versatile banner that Appazur can use across different social media platforms to improve the awareness portion of the sales funnel .

Design Problem/Challenge

Appazur's branding across different social media platforms was not only inconsistent, but the branding that did exist didn't communicate the value that the product's features delivered, and in some cases, was nothing more than a stock photo.
