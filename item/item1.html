Shopify Link

2nd Place Finalist at NBTC eCommerce Case Competition

Business Strategy, Prototyping, UI/UX Design

Overview

The Project

Build a tagible solution that Shopify can realistically implement to make it easier for aspirational entrepreneurs to succeed.

Design Problem/Challenge

While most cases tend to provide a number of facts that tend to guide competitors towards a certain type of solution, this particular Shopify case was completely open ended.

Perhaps a reflection of the fact that Shopify's eCommerce platform is already incredibly comprehensive, nevertheless, we identified a "procurement gap" that will be explained in detail. Shopify LINK is the solution to that gap.

User Research

Identifying the Procurement Gap

Once entrepreneurs have a product ready to sell, Shopify's eCommerce platform already includes a number of solutions for downstream activities. Of particular note, their acquisition of marketing chatbot AI "Kit", and the Shopify Capital program help enable even those who might be short on marketing expertise and money.

But, if we were to look upstream to procurement and ideation, we find out that Shopify doesn't actually have solutions to help entrepreneurs complete the first steps of procuring a product to sell.

Quantifying the Issue

Although there are solutions such as manufacturing directories like Alibaba, and other sourcing companies, our research shows that the progress of procurement largely remains challenging, confusing, and complicated.

Feature Definition.

Feature definition was largely driven by market research with entrepreneurs familiar with different procurement methods (e.g. drop shipping, manufacture to order, etc.) within our personal network. The Value Proposition Canvas was used to guide and collect information as shown below.
